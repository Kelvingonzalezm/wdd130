Immortalize Your Rafting Experience At Our
Different Locations

Enjoying a water rafting experience with your family just got better. You get a
10% discount on tickets for family tickets of 3 persons and above, and a
further 5% discount for all your purchases in the park if you proceed to buy your
ticket online now by clicking on "Click to book" button. At Rafting Adventure
rafting Adventure, your adventure begins with a gondola ride to the Park,
Then you and your family get to enjoy cave tours, thrills rides and lots of other
children's activities including a group dining with other families. Our gift shops
around the family are full of original and fun souvenirs to enhance your
visits and help you keep the memories forever.

Rogue River offers 10 NATURAL hot sprins soaking pools and a freshwater
pool with jetted spa. With our reduced capacity, you will be able to soak in a
personal rafting experience or enjoy it with other small group of adults. Rogue
River offers a gentle ride through calm hot waters. It's Adults only meaning
Children are not allowed here. Reservations are made at least 2 weeks before
visits. Tickets are currently available at a 5% discount for intending tourist who
book online.

If rush rush, shouting and hot vibes are your thing, then look no further than our
Rapids Fall. This location is reserved for the strong hearted and is one of the
places where you get to feel first hand, the maximum excitment and fun of water
rafting. Its never a dull moment here. Rapids Falls takes you to the extremity of
water rafting. Here, you get toride through some of the biggest rapids around in
the smallest raft where your guide depends solely on you. You will run along
some of the fastest rapids including the great Tuttl's Tumble, tombstone etc. Just
as soon as the rapids ends and you think it's over, we take you back again even to
a more challenging line. The smaller rafts here makes the rapids more
challenging and are perfect for small groups of 3 to 6. Thinking of coming here,
do keep in mind that Rapids Fall is only appropriate for skilled swimmers as
flips and involuntary swims are as common as dirts here. Tickets are available
anytime for purchase both online and in person.
